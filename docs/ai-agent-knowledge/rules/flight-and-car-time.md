# Flight and Car Time Rules

> **Current facts** (flight schedules, exact arrival times) change seasonally — always web-search before quoting. The rules below are structural; the times are approximate references.

## Airline Arrival Patterns (Chiang Mai, CNX)

| Airline | Route | Typical arrival | Day 1 touring available? |
|---------|-------|-----------------|--------------------------|
| 長榮航空 (EVA Air) | TPE → CNX | Morning (~10:00–11:00) | Yes — depart to hotel, then 2–3 stops afternoon |
| 華航 (China Airlines) | TPE → CNX | Morning (~10:00) | Yes — same as 長榮 |
| 亞洲航空 (AirAsia) | KUL hub or direct | Often late night (22:00–23:00) | No — rest day; Grab or point transfer recommended |
| 星宇航空 (Starlux) | TPE → CNX | Afternoon (~17:00) | Marginal — hotel check-in + nearby dinner only; no full touring |
| 泰國航空 (Thai Airways) | BKK hub | Variable | Depends on connection; confirm |
| 曼谷航空 (Bangkok Airways) | BKK hub | Variable | Depends; confirm |

**Rule**: When the arrival is after 17:00, Day 1 is effectively a rest/transfer day. Do not schedule major attractions.

## Departure Day Patterns

| Departure time | Recommended Day N schedule |
|----------------|---------------------------|
| Before 07:00 | No morning touring; airport transfer departs 04:00–05:00 |
| 07:00–10:00 | Airport transfer only; luggage pickup ~2h before flight |
| 10:00–14:00 | Light morning stop (1 stop max, near airport or en route) |
| After 14:00 | Half-day touring possible; confirm with team |

**Rule**: Point transfer (接送機) is typically priced as one-day car fee. See quote-included-excluded.md for pricing exceptions.

## Car Time Standards

### Chiang Mai (清邁)
- **Standard day**: 10 hours car time included.
- Typical depart 08:30 → return 18:30.
- City circuit stops within 10h easily for 3–4 attractions.

### Chiang Rai (清萊)
- **Standard day**: 12 hours car time included.
- Departure by 07:00–07:30 from Chiang Mai hotel.
- Allows: White Temple (白廟), Blue Temple (藍廟), Baan Dam (黑屋), Golden Triangle (金三角), optional Singha Park (幸福農場) on the way back.
- Return to Chiang Mai by 19:00–20:00.

### Doi Inthanon Day
- ~1.5h each way from Chiang Mai city.
- Full day itinerary: twin pagodas, summit, waterfall, lunch.
- Total car time ~10–11h; standard 10h rate applies; 1h overtime possible.

## Point Transfer Pricing

- Airport or train station pickup/dropoff on arrival or departure day.
- Usually priced at one-day car fee (same as a full charter day).
- **Exception cases requiring manual judgment**: mountain resort (e.g., Doi Inthanon resort), elephant camp resort, or any dropoff > 45 min from Chiang Mai city that returns same-day.
- If the same day includes both a point transfer AND significant touring, charge as a full-day charter, not a point transfer.
