# Family Pacing Rules

> **Scope**: Applies to all Chiangway multi-day family itineraries. The driver+guide team separation means the guide can manage energy levels independently — still follow these limits.
>
> **Note**: These are operational guidelines from team experience. Always flag unusual cases to Eric.

## Child Age Tiers

| Age | Tier | Key constraints |
|-----|------|-----------------|
| 0–2 | Infant | Mandatory car seat, no night activities after 20:00, max 2 attraction stops/day, afternoon nap window 13:00–15:00 |
| 3–5 | Toddler | Car seat recommended, no sustained walking > 1.5h, reduce crowded/loud venues, avoid water park + night safari same day |
| 6–10 | Active child | Can handle 3–4 stops/day, standard pace OK, water activities possible |
| 11+ | Teen | Near-adult pace; still flag very long drives |

## Day Length Guidelines

- **Standard day**: depart 08:30–09:00, return by 19:00–20:00. Maximum 10 hours including transfers.
- **Early flight day** (長榮/華航): depart directly from airport; Day 1 touring starts after 10:00 is fine.
- **Late arrival day** (亞航 often 22:00+, 星宇 often 17:00+): Day 1 is a rest/point-transfer day; do NOT schedule full touring.
- **No touring before 09:00** except airport pickup.
- **Maximum consecutive touring days without a half-rest day**: 4 days (3 days for infants/toddlers).

## Activity Combination Warnings

These combinations on the same day require explicit team confirmation:

- Water park (流水樂園) + Night Safari (夜間動物園): HIGH RISK for children under 7 (fatigue + evening safety).
- Early morning flight (before 06:00) + any touring same day: requires extra rest buffer.
- Elephant camp full day + any other major stop same day: physically demanding for all ages.
- Doi Inthanon full day + another long drive: total car time may exceed 10h.
- Night market (until 22:00+) + early morning next day (before 08:00): insufficient rest for young children.

## Drive Time Limits

- **Chiang Mai city circuit**: ≤ 1h between stops is normal.
- **Chiang Mai to Doi Inthanon**: ~1.5h each way.
- **Chiang Mai to Doi Suthep**: ~40 min.
- **Chiang Mai to Elephant Nature Park**: ~1h.
- **Chiang Mai to Chiang Rai**: ~3h direct highway or ~2.5h expressway. Total car day ≥ 12h.
- For families with infants, add 30 min buffer per 2h of driving (rest/feed stop).

## Rest Stop Policy

- Every 2h of continuous driving: one 15–20 min rest stop is standard.
- Meals count as rest stops only if seated for ≥ 30 min.
- Highway rest stops are acceptable; scenic overlooks are preferred when available.

## Night Activity Cutoff

| Child age | Recommended cutoff | Hard limit |
|-----------|--------------------|------------|
| 0–2 | 19:00 | 20:00 |
| 3–5 | 20:00 | 21:00 |
| 6–10 | 21:00 | 22:00 |
| 11+ | 22:00 | 23:00 |

Night Safari (夜間動物園) typically ends around 22:00. Confirm with family before booking if children are under 5.

## Pacing Labels (used in itinerary review)

- **輕鬆 (Light)**: ≤ 2 major stops/day, no night activities, lodging check-in by 17:00.
- **一般 (Normal)**: 3–4 stops/day, optional evening activity once per trip.
- **排滿 (Full)**: 4–5 stops/day, evening activity most nights. Only for families who explicitly request it AND have no children under 6.

## Flags to Surface in Review

Always flag in itinerary review:
- Any child under 3 in an itinerary with night safari or late-evening activities.
- Any day with > 5 planned stops.
- Any day with combined car time > 10h (清邁) or > 12h (清萊).
- Missing child ages when activity mix suggests young children (安全座椅 mentioned, water park, elephant camp).
